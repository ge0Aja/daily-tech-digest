_Indiana, Utah, Washington Restrict AI in Health Insurance Claim Decisions_

_Built In_ | [Read more](https://builtin.com/articles/tech-laws-2026)

Three US states enacted laws in Q1 2026 prohibiting health insurers from using AI as the sole basis for denying or modifying claims, requiring a human in the loop. The laws reflect accelerating legislative concern about opaque algorithmic decision-making in high-stakes domains. Engineers building AI for healthcare or insurance now face concrete compliance requirements around explainability and mandatory human oversight — a pattern likely to expand to other verticals and states.
