### EU Cyber Resilience Act: Mandatory Vulnerability Reporting Kicks In September 11, 2026
**Source:** European Commission | [Read more](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/cyber-resilience-act)

The EU Cyber Resilience Act's first enforcement milestone arrives September 11, 2026: manufacturers of products with digital elements must notify cybersecurity authorities of actively exploited vulnerabilities. Full obligations follow in December 2027. Open-source stewards face lighter requirements (security policy documentation and coordinated disclosure), but commercial distributors of OSS components bear full compliance responsibility. This fundamentally shifts supply-chain security liability across the European software ecosystem and will affect any team shipping software into EU markets.
