## Security & Privacy

### Bitwarden CLI Supply Chain Attack Targeted AI Coding Tools for 93 Minutes
**Source:** The Hacker News | [Read more](https://thehackernews.com/2026/04/bitwarden-cli-compromised-in-ongoing.html)

A malicious version of `@bitwarden/cli@2026.4.0` was live on npm for 93 minutes on April 22, injected via a compromised GitHub Action in Bitwarden's CI/CD pipeline. The malware stole GitHub/npm tokens, SSH keys, `.env` files, shell history, and cloud secrets — and specifically hunted for authenticated AI coding tools including Claude Code, Cursor, Codex CLI, Aider, and Gemini CLI. Part of a broader Checkmarx supply chain campaign, Bitwarden confirmed no end-user password vault data was accessed and deprecated the malicious release promptly.

### LMDeploy SSRF Flaw (CVE-2026-33626) Exploited Within 13 Hours of Disclosure
**Source:** The Hacker News | [Read more](https://thehackernews.com/2026/04/lmdeploy-cve-2026-33626-flaw-exploited.html)

CVE-2026-33626 (CVSS 7.5) is an SSRF vulnerability in LMDeploy's vision-language image loader that lets attackers probe internal networks, AWS IMDS, Redis, and MySQL — and exfiltrate data via DNS. Sysdig detected active exploitation on a honeypot just 12 hours 31 minutes after public disclosure on April 21. The attacker rotated between VLMs (InternVL, internlm-xcomposer2) during an 8-minute recon session to evade detection. All versions ≤0.12.0 with VLM support are affected; patch available in LMDeploy 0.12.3.

### Vercel Customer Credentials Exposed via Cascading OAuth Attack Through Context.ai
**Source:** The Hacker News | [Read more](https://thehackernews.com/2026/04/vercel-breach-tied-to-context-ai-hack.html)

A breach at analytics platform Context.ai enabled a Google Workspace takeover at Vercel, exposing limited customer credentials through a cascading OAuth chain attack. Attackers leveraged OAuth token theft from the Context.ai compromise to gain unauthorized access to Vercel's internal systems. The incident is a sharp reminder that third-party SaaS integrations and OAuth trust chains in developer tooling ecosystems are high-value lateral movement targets.
