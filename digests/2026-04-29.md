## Cloud & Infrastructure

### Kubernetes v1.36 "Haru": User Namespaces GA, Ingress NGINX Officially Retired
**Source:** Kubernetes.io | [Read more](https://kubernetes.io/blog/2026/04/22/kubernetes-v1-36-release/)

Kubernetes v1.36 ships 70 enhancements: native user namespaces reach GA (rootless containers without third-party tooling via `hostUsers: false`), CEL-based mutating admission policies eliminate external webhook servers, and OCI image volumes are now stable. HPA scale-to-zero is enabled by default after a decade in beta. Critical removals: the gitRepo volume plugin is gone, IPVS in kube-proxy is removed, and Ingress NGINX has been formally retired with no further security updates — clusters relying on it need an immediate migration plan.

### EU Cyber Resilience Act Enforcement Escalates: Open Source Components Now a Legal Liability
**Source:** OpenSSF | [Read more](https://openssf.org/newsletter/2026/04/21/openssf-newsletter-april-2026/)
