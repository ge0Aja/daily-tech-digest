**Source:** The Hacker News / CISA | [Read more](https://thehackernews.com/2026/05/on-prem-microsoft-exchange-server-cve.html)

A high-severity (CVSS 8.1) stored cross-site scripting vulnerability in Microsoft Exchange's Outlook Web Access (OWA) is under active exploitation.
