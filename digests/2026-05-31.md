## Security & Privacy

### PAN-OS GlobalProtect CVE-2026-0257 Actively Exploited — CISA Adds to KEV on May 29
**Source:** The Hacker News / Rapid7 | [Read more](https://thehackernews.com/2026/05/pan-os-globalprotect-authentication.html)

CVE-2026-0257 is an authentication bypass in PAN-OS GlobalProtect triggered when devices reuse the authentication override certificate with another service (e.g., the HTTPS portal) — a common misconfiguration. An unauthenticated remote attacker can forge session cookies and establish unauthorized VPN connections. Exploitation was first observed May 17 from Vultr-hosted infrastructure, with a second wave on May 21. CISA added it to the Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog on May 29. Mitigation: issue a dedicated certificate exclusively for the authentication override feature, or disable the feature entirely.
